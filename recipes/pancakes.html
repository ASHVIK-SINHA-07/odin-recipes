Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft and fluffy breakfast treats served with syrup, fruits, or butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Heat a pan.
  3. Pour batter onto the pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden.
  5. Serve with syrup.
