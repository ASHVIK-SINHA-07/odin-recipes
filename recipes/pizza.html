Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular dish consisting of a baked dough base topped with sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve.
